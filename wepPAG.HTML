Welcome to A.I.F.P by WMN
Innovative Fire Protection Systems Designed with AI

Developed by Waseem Mustafah Njajreh, A.I.F.P utilizes artificial intelligence to automatically design fire protection systems including suppression, alarms, evacuation, and early detection, adhering to Saudi Building Code (SBC) and NFPA standards.

Our primary goal is to enhance fire safety through advanced technology and creativity.

Design Capabilities: Supports AutoCAD, Revit, and scripting for PDF and similar engineering software, enabling both 2D and 3D outputs based on user inputs and commands.
Simulation: Generates 3D plans and video simulations of fire suppression systems, demonstrating individual and collective functionality, and real fire scenario simulations.
